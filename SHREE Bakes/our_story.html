Our story

Baked with love, since day one

From a small home kitchen to a name people trust for fresh, honest baking.

How it all started

It began in a small home kitchen with one oven, a handful of trusted family recipes, and a lot of late nights. Shree baked for birthdays, festivals, and neighbors who kept asking for "just one more box." What was meant to be a hobby quickly turned into something people looked forward to every week.

Word spread the old fashioned way, one satisfied customer telling another. Soon, orders were coming in faster than a single home kitchen could keep up with.

Growing with care

As demand grew, so did we, but never at the cost of quality. We moved into a proper baking space, brought in a small team who shared the same values, and started sourcing better ingredients directly from trusted local suppliers.

Every new recipe still goes through the same process it always has: tested at home first, tasted by family and friends, and only added to the menu once it feels exactly right.

Where we are today

Today, SHREE Bakes serves thousands of happy customers, but we still bake in small batches, by hand, the same way we did on day one. No shortcuts, no preservatives, just fresh ingredients and recipes that have been loved for years.

Every cake, cookie, and pastry that leaves our kitchen carries the same intention it did at the very beginning: to bring a little joy and a little home into your day.

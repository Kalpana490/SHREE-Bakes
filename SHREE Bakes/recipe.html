What goes into our bakes

Real ingredients, honest process

No shortcuts, no preservatives, just good baking done right.

Farm-sourced flour

We work directly with local mills to source flour that's fresh, unbleached, and free from artificial additives.

Fresh dairy, daily

Butter, cream, and cheese are delivered fresh each morning, never frozen or stored for long stretches.

No preservatives

Everything we bake is made to be eaten fresh, not shelf-stable. What you taste is what actually went in.

Natural flavoring

Real vanilla pods, natural cocoa, and fresh fruit, never artificial extracts or flavor concentrates.

How we bake

Clean kitchen, careful hands

Every batch is baked in a sanitized kitchen with strict hygiene checks, from raw ingredient to final pack.

Sanitized workstations before every single batch

Gloves and hairnets worn throughout baking and packing

Ingredients stored and tracked at the right temperature

Sealed, food-safe packaging on every order

More than just baking

SHREE Bakes was built on a simple idea: food made at home, with care, always tastes better. That belief still shapes every decision we make, from the ingredients we choose to the way each order is packed.

We're not chasing shortcuts or mass production. We're a small team that still checks every batch by hand, because that's the only way we know how to bake.

"We treat every kitchen step like it's for our own family."
